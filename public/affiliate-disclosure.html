Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 8, 2026

How We Earn Revenue

Anara Gifts is a paid app that also participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you purchase a product through a link in the app, we may earn a small commission from the retailer at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate Partners

We currently work with the following affiliate programs:

  • Amazon Associates: Anara Gifts is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites and apps to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.
  • eBay Partner Network: We participate in the eBay Partner Network and may earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our links to eBay.com.
  • Etsy Affiliate Program: We participate in the Etsy affiliate program and may earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our links to Etsy.com.
  • Awin: We participate in the Awin affiliate network and may earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our links to partner retailers.

How This Affects Recommendations

Our gift recommendations are generated by AI based on the interests, preferences, and signals you provide about the people in your life. Affiliate partnerships do not influence which products are recommended. We recommend what we believe is the best gift — not the product that pays the highest commission.

Your Cost

Clicking an affiliate link and purchasing a product costs you nothing extra. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer. Affiliate commissions are paid by the retailer, not by you.

Why We Disclose This

We believe in transparency. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) requires disclosure of affiliate relationships, and we're happy to comply. If you have questions about our affiliate partnerships, contact us at support@anaragifts.app.
