Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Anara: Gifts They'll Love ("Anara Gifts") collects the following data to provide gift recommendation services:

  • Account information: Name and email address via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In.
  • Contacts: Names and birthdays only, imported with your explicit permission. We do not access phone numbers, emails, or addresses.
  • Gift signals: Notes, links, and photos you voluntarily capture about gift ideas.
  • Gift history: Records of gifts you log, including price and satisfaction ratings.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens for sending reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized gift recommendations using AI.
  • To send occasion reminders and gift suggestions via push notifications.
  • To improve recommendation quality over time based on your feedback.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Anara Gifts:

  • Supabase: Authentication, database, and backend infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI-powered signal processing and gift recommendations. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Associates: Product links and affiliate recommendations. Amazon Privacy Notice
  • Expo: Push notification delivery. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL databases with row-level security policies. All data is encrypted in transit via TLS. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can export all your data from the Settings screen at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Anara Gifts is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
