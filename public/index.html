Why I built this

Every time a friend's birthday came around, I'd panic-browse Amazon the night before and end up with something generic. The frustrating part? I'd remember them mentioning the perfect thing weeks earlier — but by then it was gone from my memory. I built Anara to be the app I wished I had: a place to save those little hints as they happen, so when the occasion comes, you already know exactly what to get.

— Abhi Joshi
